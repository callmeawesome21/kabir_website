About.

I've always been artistically inclined. Throughout preschool and kindergarten, I enjoyed arts and crafts and eventually learned how to draw, creating drawings of some of my favorite characters. I remember a day when I was home from daycare, I used watercolor to portray Thomas the Train, who at the time was my favorite character. Little did I know that this small piece foreshadowed how I continued to create until now. I am currently 16 years old, a high school student and my artwork still revolves around characters I like. In elementary school, I became interested in Marvel and DC movies and was heavily intrigued by the characters portrayed in them. A lot of my pieces are inspired by these characters, and I continue to create artwork involving them.

My artwork also involves a lot of my imagination. I consider myself creative and to envision my thoughts, I often express them through different types of digital media depending on the thought. Looking at the gallery, for example, you can see the concept of an instant noodle brand that I created or a representation of a “sharkodile”, a hybrid animal that I thought of.

This website is my art portfolio, where I post the highlights of my artwork in the “Gallery.” They are uploaded chronologically, with the most recent ones at the top. At the beginning of my journey, I used many physical mediums, such as paint and pencils, but more recently, I switched to digital art, which I create through Adobe Photoshop.

"Art is the journey of my imagination, where every stroke tells a story and every character brings my dreams to life."

- Kabir
